This is the about page

The Intelligent Library System is a web application that is designed to make the management of library resources, such as books, journals and multimedia content, more efficient and significantly increase the efficiency of its librarians. This system is divided into two parts. One is the user interface, which allows user to log in to their personal accounts and take various operations, such as borrowing books, returning books, extending loan periods and reporting book defects. The other part is the administrator interface, where the administrator can manage all student accounts and maintenance of all the books in the library. In addition, the system helps administrators to check the number and status of books in each section and assign staff to deal with book defects issues reported by students.
